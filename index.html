Where does it go?

All donations and profits from sold tshirts will be used to provide supplies/housing to underprivileged children with Autism in India. Our current goal is to provide their school in India (excessively hot weather year-round) with air-conditioning!

Monica, Founder & Web Designer

Monica Muthuraj is a senior at JP Stevens High School in Edison, New Jersey. After working with impoverished children at a number of schools in India every year, she noticed that children with Autism especially struggled without special care and adequate housing. She founded Tie Dye for Autism in 2015 to provide orphaned/underpriveleged children with Autism Spectrum Disorder with essential resources and decent living conditions.
